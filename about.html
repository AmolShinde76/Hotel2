Luxury Without Compromise in the Heart of Sangli

Welcome to Hotel Swami, an urban paradise that blends luxury and comfort. We are not just a place to stay, but a destination that creates unforgettable memories.

Strategically located in the heart of Jakarta, Hotel Swami is a stunning modern oasis that blends contemporary architecture with a touch of classic Indonesian elegance.

Every corner of Hotel Swami is designed to indulge your senses. From the grand lobby to the elegant rooms, we ensure that every detail reflects our 5-star standards.

Why is Hotel Swami Special?

  • Lokasi Strategis Located in the heart of Sangli, close to business centers and major tourist destinations. Enjoy easy access to the entire city.
  • World-Class Facilities Indulge yourself in our luxurious spa, swim in the infinity pool with a city view, or stay fit at our state-of-the-art fitness center.
